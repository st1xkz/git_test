Caramel latte

You can make this caramel coffee latte recipe right at home.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon sugar-free caramel topping
  • 1 tablespoon caramel sauce
  • 1/4 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup coffee

Directions

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Combine milk and brown sugar in a saucepan over medium heat. Heat until frothy and close to simmering, about 3 minutes.
  3. Reduce heat to low; whisk in caramel sauce and vanilla.
  4. Stir in coffee.
